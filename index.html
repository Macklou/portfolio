Me

I am a researcher and consultant specialising in digital media, art, and culture with a focus on global cultural dynamics. Over the years, I have partnered with universities and academic institutions to explore and analyse the evolving digital culture landscape and its impact on society. My work includes writing and presenting on a wide range of cultural topics, as well as contributing to publications and platforms. In addition to my research and writing, I am passionate about public speaking, sharing knowledge and engaging audiences on topics related to culture, technology, and globalisation. I also work directly with organisations, providing tailored consultancy services to help them navigate cultural complexities and leverage digital innovation for growth and societal impact. Please feel free to explore my website and CV, and contact me to say hi.
